Visiting Here

The Museum has an easy approach by road and rail. The important railway Stations Kachiguda and Nampally are within three miles. Road transport buses run frequently from all parts of the city to Afzalgunj which is within walking distance from the museum.

Request to the visitors!!

  • Fee entry to Children upto 5 years of age.
  • No Smartphones (mobiles) are allowed inside the Museum without purchase of a photography ticket.
  • children upto 5 years free School children upto 18 years with id card/Authorisation letter allowed free entry
  • All Smart phone users are requested to purchase the mobile ticket,Penality Rs. 500/- will be charged for using mobile without ticket.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited.
  • Avail the cloak room facility located near the booking counter for depositing luggage.
  • Do not touch the objects on display as they are prone to disfigurement.
  • Try to avoid use of cell phone in the gallery and taking selfies.
  • Do not carry notified objects such as knives, scissors, blades, razors, match boxes or lighters.
  • Cooperate with the security personnel.
  • Please use garbage box and keep the surroundings neat and clean.
  • Do not use flash light in the gallery.
